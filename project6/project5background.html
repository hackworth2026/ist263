About Fragrances

Fragrance Management

Owning cologne can set a strong precedent in your mind, as well as in others. That being, you hold a strong value in the image you make of yourself and the attraction that you wish to appease. And one notable thing about colognes is they are expensive as they come. So with the ownership of cologne you have to master utilizing the least to make the most.

Now, here is some additional products and procedures to purchase and complete before using your cologne:

•First, the product that you would want to purchase before applying your cologne is lotion. As moisturized skin is proven to hold fragrant scents for a longer period then dry skin.

•Next, take a shower to remove any old and less than preferred scents. Then, completely dry yourself. After this we will apply lotion to your body, more specifically, hitting your pressure points. These pressure points are key spots to spray your cologne. And the great thing about these colognes and a important fact, most only need you to spray it once.

Fragance Cost

When purchasing your first cologne, you are introduced to a new world of collectibles. And the longevity of these collectibles can last a good while, depending on how you treat them. To elaborate, according to BYRDIE (https://www.byrdie.com), “Most fragrance manufacturers will recommend tossing your bottle after anywhere from one to three years (check your label), but since fragrance doesn't expire in the same sense that food does, it's sometimes okay to keep using a bottle for four, even five years”. Now on the cost side of this collection, the prices can range in many different amounts. As this value is correlated to your desires in cologne, for example; a desire for higher quality and sophisticated cologne will be more expensive than its opposite.
